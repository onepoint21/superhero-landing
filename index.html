What we're doing.
Habitat Protection & Expansion
Providing safe, protected land where wolves can roam freely without the threat of habitat destruction or human interference.
Breeding & Genetic Diversity
Carefully managing breeding programs to ensure a healthy, genetically diverse wolf population for future generations.
Rehabilitation & Release
Rescuing injured or orphaned wolves, providing medical care, and preparing them for release back into the wild.
Education & Advocacy
Raising awareness about wolf conservation through education, outreach programs, and advocacy for better wildlife policies.
